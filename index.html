ROMANIAN RECIPES

I`am Romanian as some of you may know. This means I have a whole Rolodex filled with recipes from the motherland. If you're not familiar with Romanian cuisine then you're in for a treat. This is because Romanian food is filled with intense flavors a nd simple ingredients. And did I mention that it's comfort food to its core?

These dishes are typically built around the star ingredient. Ingredients like chicken, pork, or beef. We add in lots of different veggies and spices because we like to bulk up our soups and stews. From cabbage rolls to Pasca bread. From Romanian Meatball Soup to Stuffed Peppers. Whatever it may be that catches your eye scrolling down, you're guaranteed to love it.
